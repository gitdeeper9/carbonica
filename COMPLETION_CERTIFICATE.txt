╔════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║                         PROJECT DESCRIPTION                                ║
╠════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╣
║                                                                            ║
║   CARBONICA (Carbon Accounting and Regulatory Budget Observatory for       ║
║   Networked Integrated Carbon Assessment) is a physically rigorous         ║
║   eight-parameter framework for real-time quantification of global        ║
║   carbon cycle dynamics, natural sink capacity, and the critical          ║
║   threshold at which Earth's self-regulating biogeochemical systems       ║
║   approach saturation.                                                     ║
